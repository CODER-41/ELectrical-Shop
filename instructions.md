Project : Electronics Shop
From time to time individuals all over the world need to either replace electronic appliances or buy new electronic appliances.

Solution
Create a simple ecommerce website that will have a main aim of selling electronics to customers. The electronic products sold should be delivered to the customer upon order purchase and completion.

MVP Features
Login
Create an account
Listing of electronic items based on categories
Cart section for viewing all electronic  products added to cart
Checkout section for order purchase 
Simulate a payment process Internally by generating address, billing information and invoices for every order submitted.

Admin Features
Perform CRUD operations of electonic products.
Add users based on roles to manage electronic  products and order made.
View analytics of different electonic products within the platform 
View analytics of Orders made by customers within the platform
